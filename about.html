About


Entrepreneurship Club (E-CLUB) aims to serve the broad range of both graduate and undergraduate students at The University of Florida seeking to engage in the topic of Entrepreneurship, Creativity, and Innovation in educative and social capacities by fostering a strong network of like-minded individuals from which value will be added to all members.

We aim to serve this need by providing a prominent and diverse speaker series, creative workshops, monthly socials & networking events, numerous startup events like Startup Weekend and 3 Day Startup, venture pitch competitions, hack-a-thons, mentoring and coaching by successful entrepreneurs, introductions to investors and service providers, and immersion in the Gainesville innovation ecosystem & bursting startup community.

We want to bring together people from across campus in every discipline, from liberal arts and sciences to journalism and sociology to engineering and everything in between designing, marketing and advertising. All of these are important when forming successful startup companies.

From the minute you walk in the door to one of our meetings or events you will feel the energy! We are friendly and welcoming group of students excited about growing our organization!

Are there any leadership opportunities for you to get involved in to gain experience and boost your resume? YES!

What are you interested in? We have it all! Operations, management, marketing, finance, strategy, planning, social media guru's, technology, photography, videography, bloggers, graphic designers, events, social butterflies, fundraising, community service, networking in Gainesville or on campus!
