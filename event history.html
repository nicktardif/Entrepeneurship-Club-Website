iBeautifyMe

Date

June 2, 2014

Speaker

Danielle Drake,

Founder

A life-long devotee of all things beauty, Danielle Drake trained as a makeup artist at Cosmix Makeup Academy, in addition to training in virtually every aspect of the beauty industry, from aesthetics to brand management and sales.

Her constant contact with women (and the occasional man) seeking advice from her, during her work with SmashBox Cosmetics and on the sales floor at Sephora, inspired her to create iBeautifyMe.

Driven, in part, by questions from thousands of women who wanted to know where to find a good colorist, a stylist who understood how to cut curly hair, where to get the best spray tans, and where to go for facials, she realized social media provided guidance on everything from restaurants to mechanics—but nothing on services by members of the beauty trade. As a freelance makeup artist—and someone who clearly knew where to go and what to buy—Danielle understood her role as an influencer, backed by an education in marketing, could connect a world of aesthetics services to women changing jobs, moving to new locations, or just looking for better services than they’d already received.
